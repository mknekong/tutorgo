    About

    About Us

    TutorGo is an IT company based in Kohima, Nagaland focused in providing connecting link between students and tutors for private tuition. Many students are not able to find a suitable tutor for private tuition in subjects which he/she is dedicated to learn. There are also many educated unemployed in Nagaland and are willing to work but cannot find a good platform suitable with their educational qualification. Thus, TutorGo is an educational based company which will help the students get the right mentor for their studies and also provide job opportunities to educated Unemployed Youth of Nagaland.

    Through our platform students will be able find the best tutor according to his/her requirements in and around his/her area. It will also serve as a register for the tutor to check how many students are enrolled with him depending on the classes and batch.

    Why TutorGo?

    There is a serious need for a connecting link between students and tutors for private tuition. Many students are not able to find a suitable tutor for private tuition in subjects which he/she is dedicated to learn.

    • 01 Help Students connect with tutors.

      TutorGo helps find suitable tutor for student just in one click. Students will no longer need to search for tutors, the vast variety of tutors will be available in our TutorGo platform.

    • 02 Help Tutors maintain register

      TutorGo platform will help tutors in maintain a register for students in his/her classes. This will help tutor in finding when the students have join, which batch he/she is in, etc.

    • 03 Help Parents maintaining a close relationship with tutors

      TutorGo platform will enable parents to be aware of thier childrens progress.
